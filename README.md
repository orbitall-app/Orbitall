# 🪐 Orbitall

**A free, offline personal calendar & scheduler that lives entirely on your phone.**

No account. No internet. No subscription. Add it to your home screen once and it works forever — even in airplane mode.

### ▶ Try it now: **[orbitall-app.github.io/Orbitall](https://orbitall-app.github.io/Orbitall/)**

Open that link on your phone, then **Add to Home Screen** (iPhone: Share → Add to Home Screen · Android: Chrome menu → Install App). It installs like a real app, with its own icon, and runs fully offline from then on.

---

## What it does

Orbitall was built around how people actually think about time — some things repeat, some happen once, and some don't have a date yet.

- **Recurring schedule** — bills, payments, and routines set up once: monthly by date, monthly by weekday (e.g. *first Wednesday*), weekly, every two weeks, or yearly. Optional dollar amounts get tallied for the month.
- **One-time tasks** — tap any day to add something; it auto-clears after the day passes.
- **Multi-day events** — vacations and trips as a single event across a date range.
- **Notes** — jot undated thoughts, then assign a date to turn one into a real task.
- **Quick Add** — type naturally: *"june 5 dentist at 2:30pm"*, *"every other friday paycheck"*, *"vacation june 21-29"*. Voice input works offline too.
- **Details field** — add notes, addresses, or confirmation numbers to anything.
- **Search** — find any entry by keyword across the whole app.
- **List view** — everything coming up, paged in 30-day windows you can swipe through.
- **Share** — send selected days to a friend as a plain text code; they review and approve what to import. No files, no accounts.
- **Custom colors & labels** — six built-in colors plus unlimited custom ones, each nameable ("Bills", "Gym", "Kids").
- **20+ themes** — from clean Dark/Light to glass gradients (Sunset, Aurora, Mountain) and creative looks (Matrix, Spooky, Synthwave, and more).
- **Backup & restore** — export your data to a file anytime.

## Privacy

Everything is stored locally on your device. No account, no cloud, no tracking, no ads — your data never leaves your phone.

## Tech

A single self-contained HTML file: vanilla JavaScript, no frameworks, no dependencies, no build step. Installable as a PWA on iOS and Android.
